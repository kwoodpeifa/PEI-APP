Advance Payments Program
Prince Edward Island

Providing PEI producers with low-cost operating capital through the federal Agricultural Marketing Programs Act.

Interest-Free

Access the first $250,000 of your advance interest-free, supported by Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada.

Prime - 0.75%

Additional funds up to $1,000,000 are available at a preferred rate below prime for all PEIFA members.

On-Island Support

Local administration ensures your paperwork and inspections are handled by people who know PEI farming.

Program Timeline

Key dates and responsibilities for the production year.

01

1 Spring Setup

Applications open April 1st. Submit your signed Repayment Agreement and production estimates to initiate your advance.

02

2 Sales Cycle

As you sell your product, submit proof of sale. Repayments are due within 30 days of receiving payment from your buyer.

03

3 Final Settlement

The production year ends September 30th. All advances must be cleared or accounted for via storage reports by this date.

Eligible Commodities

We provide advances for a vast range of PEI agricultural products, both storable and livestock.

Crops & Products

  • Potatoes (Fresh & Seed)
  • Grains (Wheat, Barley, Oats, Soy)
  • Fruits (Apples, Strawberries, Cranberries)
  • Vegetables (Carrots, Onions, etc.)
  • Turf Grass & Flowers

Livestock

Standard Flow

Mainly for beef cattle (cow-calf). This allows for longer production cycles with flexible repayment.

Continuous Flow

For hogs, lambs, and feeder cattle where inventory is constantly moving. Settlement occurs at year-end.

Business Risk Management (BRM) Requirements

All advances must be secured. Crops typically require **AgriInsurance** (Crop Insurance) coverage, while livestock advances are usually secured via **AgriStability** enrollment.

Fees & Eligibility

Who Can Apply?

Canadian citizens or permanent residents

Sole proprietors, partnerships, or corporations

Producers responsible for the marketing of the product

Must be enrolled in AgriInsurance or AgriStability
