Federal Support Program

Financial Support for
Island Farmers

Access up to $1,000,000 in low-cost operating capital through the Advance Payments Program.

Eligible Products

The program covers nearly all agricultural products in Prince Edward Island. Advances are generally calculated at 50% of the market value.

Crops & Specialized Production

Standard repayment window: Up to 18 months.

Livestock Models

Extended repayment: Up to 24 months for cattle.

Standard Flow

Beef Cattle (Cow/Calf)

Designed for operations where animals are raised over a long cycle. Advances are repaid as animals are sold.

Continuous Flow

Hogs, Lambs & Beef Heavies

For high-turnover operations. These advances are based on the minimum number of marketable animals on the farm at any given time.

Important Exclusion

Supply-Managed Livestock: Dairy cattle, poultry, and eggs are not eligible for the Advance Payments Program under federal regulations.

The Advance Payments Program is a federal loan guarantee program administered by the PEIFA in cooperation with Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada under the Agricultural Marketing Programs Act (AMPA).
