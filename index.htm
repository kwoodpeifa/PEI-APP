Advance Payment Program

Prince Edward Island

Financial Support for Island Farmers

Interest-Free

The first $250,000 of your advance is interest-free, funded by the federal government.

Thresholds are set annually

Interest-Advantaged

Amounts above $250,000 are provided at Prime - 0.75%, negotiated for PEI producers.

Local Guidance

PEIFA handles the paperwork, inspections, and liaison with creditors for you.
