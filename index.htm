Financial Support for Island Farmers

Access up to $1,000,000 in low-cost operating capital through the Advance Payments Program.

Interest-Free

The first $250,000 of your advance is interest-free, funded by the federal government.

Thresholds are set annually

Interest-Advantaged

Amounts above $250,000 are provided at Prime - 0.75%, negotiated for PEI producers.

Local Guidance

PEIFA handles the paperwork, inspections, and liaison with creditors for you.

The Process

Program Lifecycle & Deadlines

A roadmap for managing your capital throughout the production year.

1

Application Phase

Beginning April 1st

Submit your application with signed repayment agreements and production estimates.

Seeding Reports

May – July: Confirm planted acres via FSAR report.

2

Sales & Repayment

Ongoing / 30-Day Limit

Repay as you sell:

  • Submit proof of sale documentation
  • Pay back the portion of the advance
  • Finalize within 30 days of sale
3

Final Settlement

Production Year End

Storage Reports

Dec 31 / Jan 31: Annual storage reporting and final documentation enforcement.

Deadline

September 30

All outstanding balances must be cleared to maintain eligibility.
