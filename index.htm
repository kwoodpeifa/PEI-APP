Federal Support Program

Financial Support for
Island Farmers

Access up to $1,000,000 in low-cost operating capital through the Advance Payments Program.

Interest-Free

The first $250,000 of your advance is interest-free, funded by the federal government.

Interest-Advantaged

Amounts above $250,000 are provided at Prime - 0.75%, negotiated for PEI producers.

Local Guidance

PEIFA handles the paperwork and inspections, acting as your local liaison.

Program Lifecycle & Deadlines

A clear guide to managing your advance throughout the year.

1

Application Phase

April 1st Opening

Submit your application with signed repayment agreements and production estimates.

Seeding Report Window

May – July: Confirm planted acres via FSAR report.

2

Sales & Repayment

Ongoing / Within 30 Days

Repay as you sell:

  • • Submit proof of sale documentation
  • • Pay back the portion of the advance
  • • Must be done within 30 days of sale
3

Final Settlement

End of Production Year

Dec 31 / Jan 31

Annual storage reporting and final documentation enforcement.

September 30

Final Repayment Deadline.

All outstanding balances must be cleared by this date.
