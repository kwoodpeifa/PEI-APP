Federal Support Program

Financial Support for
Island Farmers

Access up to $1,000,000 in low-cost operating capital through the Advance Payments Program.

Interest-Free

The first $250,000 of your advance is interest-free, funded by the federal government.

Interest-Advantaged

Amounts above $250,000 are provided at Prime - 0.75%, negotiated for PEI producers.

Local Guidance

PEIFA handles the paperwork and inspections, acting as your local liaison.

Program Roadmap & Key Deadlines

Ensuring you stay compliant and funded throughout the production cycle.

Program Opening

Early April 1st

  • Submit completed APP application
  • Sign Repayment Agreement
  • Provide production estimates & required documentation
Seeding Report Window

May – July

  • Submit Final Seeded Acreage Report (FSAR), if required
  • Ensure planted acres align with initial application
Ongoing Requirement

Within 30 Days of Each Sale

  • Repay corresponding portion of advance
  • Submit Proof of Sale (Buyer, Date, Commodity, Quantity, Amount)

December 31

Annual Reporting Deadline

  • • Submit storage report for any unsold commodities
  • • Confirm inventory in storage (e.g., potatoes, grain)

January 31

Documentation Enforcement

Mandatory: All repayments must include Proof of Sale documentation. No exceptions after this date.

September 30

Final Repayment Deadline

  • • Repay entire outstanding advance balance
  • • Submit all remaining Proof of Sale documentation
  • Note: If your Production Period ends earlier, full repayment is required by that date.

Standard Flow

For hogs, lambs, and beef heavies. Settled at the end of the advance year.
